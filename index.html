About Me

I'm a designer and developer based in Mumbai — working at the intersection of visual craft and engineering. I care deeply about interfaces that feel considered: every spacing decision, every transition, every word.

My work tends toward the clean and the quiet, but I know when restraint gives way to something more expressive. I've worked with early-stage startups, agencies, and independently.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually reading about type, walking around the city looking at signage, or finding new ways to break my own grid systems.
