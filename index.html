Brand Identity System
An exploration in visual identity and brand strategy.
Product Design Branding

A complete visual identity for a Series A fintech startup — logo suite, colour palette, type system, icon library, and a component library in Figma ready for engineering handoff. The brand needed to feel trustworthy without being corporate, and approachable without being playful.

The process began with competitive auditing across the fintech landscape, identifying visual patterns to subvert. The resulting mark is a geometric form that references both data and direction, rendered in a palette that reads as confident and calm.

The type system pairs a geometric sans for headlines with a humanist serif for body copy — an unusual choice in this category that creates immediate differentiation.

E-commerce Redesign
Conversion-focused UI overhaul for an artisan marketplace.
Web Development UI Design

End-to-end redesign of an artisan goods marketplace, focusing on product discovery, checkout friction, and seller credibility signals. Resulted in a 22% increase in average order value during A/B testing.

The core insight from user interviews: buyers didn't trust the platform, they trusted the sellers. The redesign made individual maker stories more prominent, brought craft provenance forward, and reduced the platform's visual footprint in favour of the products themselves.

Checkout was simplified from seven steps to three, with persistent progress indicators and trust signals placed at key drop-off points.

Interactive Data Story
Scroll-driven narrative built with D3.js and WebGL.
MotionData Viz3D

A scroll-driven data narrative exploring urban heat islands across South Asian cities. Built with D3.js for charts and Three.js for the 3D globe, running entirely in the browser at 60fps.

The project began with 40 years of temperature records across 12 cities. The challenge was making the data emotionally legible — not just accurate. Each scroll trigger reveals a new layer of the story, moving from macro climate patterns down to street-level impact.

The 3D globe allows users to rotate and explore data points spatially, giving the audience agency over how they navigate the information.

App Onboarding Flow
Research-led redesign that cut drop-off by 38%.
UX ResearchPrototyping

Six rounds of usability testing with 42 participants to identify where first-time users dropped off. Redesigned progressive disclosure model cut drop-off by 38% and improved day-7 retention significantly.

The original onboarding asked users for 14 pieces of information before they could use the product. After mapping the actual decision points, we restructured into three stages: a two-step hook, a deferred permission ask, and a contextual setup flow triggered by first use.

Users reported feeling less pressured, more curious, and more confident in what the product would do for them.

Editorial Layout System
Modular grid and type system for a long-form digital publication.
TypographyDesign Systems

A modular twelve-column grid, a bespoke type scale, and a library of reusable editorial components for a long-form digital magazine. Designed to accommodate short news items and 10,000-word features with equal elegance.

The typographic challenge was creating a scale that worked across five different article formats — briefs, analysis, longform features, interviews, and photo essays — each with distinct reading rhythms.

The component library was built in Figma with auto-layout and variables, enabling the editorial team to compose pages without designer involvement while maintaining visual consistency.

About Me

I'm a designer and developer based in Mumbai — working at the intersection of visual craft and engineering. I care deeply about interfaces that feel considered: every spacing decision, every transition, every word.

My work tends toward the clean and the quiet, but I know when restraint gives way to something more expressive. I've worked with early-stage startups, agencies, and independently.
